# Source
IKEA assembly instructions (downloaded from official IKEA assembly guide listing).

# Tools
- Phillips screwdriver (fastens frame and hinges)
- Hammer (taps dowels and back nails)
- Allen key (5mm) (tightens cam bolts)
- Power drill with 3mm bit (pilot holes for hinges and anchors)
- Level (optional for plumb alignment)
- Stud finder (optional for wall anchoring)
- Tape measure (optional for spacing)

# Tool reasons
## Phillips screwdriver
Used for frame screws, hinge plates, and hardware fixtures.

## Hammer
Used to seat dowels and nail the back panel to square the frame.

## Power drill with 3mm bit
Used for pilot holes to prevent splitting and ensure straight anchors.

# Preparation
1. Build the frame on the floor with space to rotate upright.
2. Keep cams loose until the back panel is secured.
3. Verify the frame is square before standing upright.

# Assembly steps
1. Insert dowels, then connect side panels to top and bottom.
2. Secure the back panel; nail every 15 cm.
3. Stand the frame with help; tighten cams evenly.
4. Install hinge plates and doors after anchoring.

# Safety notes
- Anchor the wardrobe before loading shelves or doors.
- Use the correct anchor for the wall type.
